Bolognese

This will take about 45 minutes to cook

with a price of 5 dollars per portion

Ingredients:

  • 1 tomato
  • 200g pasta
  • tomato pure
  • 2 hacked tomatos in box
  • 400 g minced beef
  • 1/4 white onion

How to cook:

  1. cut the onion into fine bits
  2. browne the minced beef with the onion
  3. add tomato and tomato pure
  4. cook the pasta in water to al dente
  5. put sauce on pasta
